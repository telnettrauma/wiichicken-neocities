What Makes it Special

Nintype is a mobile keyboard. What makes it special is how customizable and fun it is, as well as how fast it will allow you to type on a phone. Nintype has so many features that I have never seen, nor expected, from a keyboard. Not only that, but also the overwhelming amount of customization really make this keyboard worth it.
You can tell I really like this keyboard, because I'm making a website that exists just to advertise it (which you're reading right now).

What are your Favorite Features?

There are SO many, but here are just a few highlights:

  • Shortcuts
  • Abundant Amount of Gestures
  • Edit bar
  • Calculator

What are the Downsides?

Well, the app hasn't been updated since 2018. The developer has teased multiple times over Twitter about possibly creating a sequel, but so far nothing has happened.
Because of this, the app is fairly buggy. Sometimes the keyboard doesn't show up, crashes are frequent, and you frequently have to switch back to it when opening a textbox.
With that said, its still worth it.

Download

On iOS, you will have to pay about $5 (American). If you're on Android, you can just download an APK (the app may have a different name on Android)
