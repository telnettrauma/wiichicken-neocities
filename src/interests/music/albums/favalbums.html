My Favorite Albums

Last updated: December 8, 2023

The Weather

Pond

By far one of the catchiest and well produced albums I have ever listened to. Every song is unique and still really good. This album also has my most listened to song, Paint Me Silver.

Plastic Beach

Gorillaz

Plastic Beach is the most well themed album. All of the songs have a really nice and consistent vibe to them that make them a treat to listen to.

Are You Living in the Real World?

Oliver Malcolm

Oliver Malcolm is severly underrated. His first EP, Are You Living in the Real World? helps prove that. Most of the songs are unique and memorable. It's unlike anything that I have ever heard.

Little Dark Age

MGMT

The first half of Little Dark Age is so fun and has so many classic songs. It's fairly accessible and has something for everyone, despite being a physc album.

Tickle Pink Hotel

EXUM

Just a really underrated album. Don't really have much else to say about it, other than check it out.

MM... FOOD

MF DOOM

This is a certified rap classic. Really good beats, good rhymes, and a really fun food theme make this album good. Can you see why I don't professionally review albums?

Wait

arlie

Another underrated and unique album. Every song here is really fun to listen to and I also really like the messages of some of the tracks.
